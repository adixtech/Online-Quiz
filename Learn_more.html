The ultimate interactive quiz platform for teachers and students.

📌 What is Quizipy?

Quizipy is a **powerful quiz platform** designed to make learning engaging and effective. Teachers can create quizzes, track student performance, and analyze results, while students can take quizzes, view scores, and improve their skills.

✨ Key Features

👨‍🏫 For Teachers

Create and manage quizzes, track student progress, and analyze performance in real-time.

👨‍🎓 For Students

Join quizzes easily, track your performance, and receive AI-powered feedback.

🛠️ How It Works

📌 Teachers

  • Sign up & create quizzes
  • Share quiz codes with students
  • Track performance with analytics

📌 Students

  • Join quizzes with a code
  • Complete quizzes before time runs out
  • Get scores & leaderboard rankings
